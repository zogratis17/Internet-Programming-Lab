Kovai Kondattam is a complete entertainment park with different rides for the children's excitement. Kovai Kondattam is located in Perur and its an eco- friendly amusement park. It is a total amusement park and is an ideal fun pack for the whole family to enjoy. The amusement park offers a unique way to celebrate all occasions, be it a birthday, anniversary or even tying the wedding knot - exciting arrangements can be tailor made to suit individual tastes. An amphi theatre specially designed to house live performance of both local and international standards is available for family and party events. Private bookings are undertaken for family functions and corporate events.
